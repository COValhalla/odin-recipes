Carbonara

Description

The original italian Carbonara is a delicious, yet simple recipe anyone of all cooking recipes can create!

Ingredients

  • Pasta - serving size 2
  • Parmesean Cheese - 50mg
  • Pancetta - 50g
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Fill up the pot per your pasta of choice's instructions. Place salt into the pot at 10 grams of salt per liter of water.
  2. While the water is heating up, begin preparing the sauce.
    1. Add two eggs to a small bowl.
    2. Add some pepper.
    3. Add some grated parmesean cheese.
    4. Lightly whisk together, then set aside.
    5. Cut the pancetta into small cubes and set aside.
  3. Cook the pasta Al Dente.
  4. While the pasta is cooking, add olive oil to a pan and begin cooking the pancetta until crispy. Try to target having the pancetta finished about 2 minutes prior to the pasta being finished.
  5. Turn down the heat on the pancetta, this step is critical for adding the sauce. Too much heat can cause the sauce mixture to cook too much, scrambling the eggs. Drain the pasta and add to the pancetta pan. Begin mixing/tossing the get the oil to coat the pasta evenly
  6. Add the sauce and mix evenly. Sprinkle generously with parmesean and enjoy!

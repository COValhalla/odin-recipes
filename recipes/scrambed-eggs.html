Scrambed Eggs

Description

A simple scrambled eggs with a unique addition of sun-fried tomatoes and artichoke hearts.

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 50g of artichoke hearts, diced
  • 100g of sun-dried tomatoes, diced
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Add the eggs, salt & pepper, and whisk together. Set aside.
  2. Add some olive oil to your pan and head on medium. Add the artichoke hearts and cook for a couple minutes, until begining to slightyly brown. Add the sun-dried tomatoes and cook unti all is lightly crisped.
  3. Add the eggs to the pan and begin scrambling as they start thicken up. Enjoy!
